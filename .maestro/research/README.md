# Research

This directory is for storing research notes, findings, and documentation related to the project.

## Purpose

- Store technical research and investigation results
- Document architectural decisions and trade-offs
- Keep reference materials and external resources
- Maintain exploration notes and prototypes

## Structure

Organize research by topic or feature:

```
research/
├── topic-name/
│   ├── findings.md
│   └── references.md
└── README.md
```

## Notes

- Keep research focused and actionable
- Link to relevant issues or specs when applicable
- Update findings as new information becomes available
